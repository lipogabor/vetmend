About Us

VetMend cooperates with established European manufacturers whose products are based on more than 20 years of experience in medical implant development and manufacturing.

The implant systems are developed in close collaboration with international veterinary specialists and produced according to human medical quality standards using high-grade materials and state-of-the-art technology.
